Q. Write a SQL statement to make a list for the salesmen who either work for one or more customers
or yet to join any of the customer. The customer, may have placed, either one or more orders on 
or above order amount 2000 and must have a grade, or he may not have placed any order to the 
associated supplier.

Ans-->

SELECT a.cust_name,a.city,a.grade, 
b.name AS "Salesman", 
c.ord_no, c.ord_date, c.purch_amt 
FROM customer a 
RIGHT OUTER JOIN salesman b 
ON b.salesman_id=a.salesman_id 
LEFT OUTER JOIN orders c 
ON c.customer_id=a.customer_id 
WHERE c.purch_amt>=2000 
AND a.grade IS NOT NULL;
