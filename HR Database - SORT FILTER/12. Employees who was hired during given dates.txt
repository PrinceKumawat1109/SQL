Q. From the following table, write a SQL query to find those employees who were hired between November 5th, 2007 and July 5th, 2009. Return full name (first and last), job id and hire date.

Ans--> 

SELECT first_name ||' '||last_name AS Full_Name, 
  job_id, hire_date 
   FROM employees 
    WHERE hire_date 
     BETWEEN '2007-11-05' AND '2009-07-05';
