Q. From the following table, write a SQL query to find the highest order 
(purchase) amount by each customer on a particular order date. Filter the 
result by highest order (purchase) amount above 2000.00. Return customer id, 
order date and maximum purchase amount.

Ans-->

SELECT customer_id,ord_date,MAX(purch_amt) 
FROM orders 
GROUP BY customer_id,ord_date 
HAVING MAX(purch_amt)>2000.00;
