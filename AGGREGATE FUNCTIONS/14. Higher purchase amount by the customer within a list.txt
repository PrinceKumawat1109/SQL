Q. From the following table, write a SQL query to find the maximum order 
(purchase) amount based on the combination of each customer and order date. 
Filter the rows for maximum order (purchase) amount is either 2000, 3000, 
5760, 6000. Return customer id, order date and maximum purchase amount.

Ans-->

SELECT customer_id,ord_date,MAX(purch_amt) 
FROM orders 
GROUP BY customer_id,ord_date 
HAVING MAX(purch_amt) IN(2000 ,3000,5760, 6000);
