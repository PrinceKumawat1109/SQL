Q. From the following table, write a SQL query to find employees who have previously worked as 'Sales Representatives'. Return all the fields of jobs.

Ans-->

SELECT * 
	FROM jobs 
		WHERE job_id IN 
		(SELECT job_id 
			FROM employees 
				WHERE employee_id IN 
        (SELECT employee_id 
			FROM job_history 
				WHERE job_id='SA_REP'));
